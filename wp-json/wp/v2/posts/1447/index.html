twitters blurred a birdsong
\nonce carried by the trees
\nblogs and vlogs and wtf’s
\nflickr’d is now the morning dew<\/p>\n

facebooks and avatars, minds in the cookie jar
\nall gung-ho at the hide-and-seek
\nonce tribes and true colours
\ngathered among us, shared stories,cuppa tea<\/p>\n

with eyes faced down a tired clown
\nwithdraws from bluescreen action
\nbattle’s done, the war is won
\nhe grabs this chance for awakening<\/p>\n

mankind circles are a breaking
\nfor nought but a fleeting pledge
\na pledge of I’ll love you or hate you, I’ll stalk or I’ll break you
\ndangling on ego’s edge<\/p>\n

bring on souls’ rising
\nbirdsongs and dew, families and stew
\nhearts and hugs and coffee mugs
\n‘click here’ for the taking<\/p>\n

but be there for the making
\nAll can be truly done<\/p>\n

2018.02.20, Antwerp – dedicated to Charissa and Nigel<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
